Our Story

Founded with a passion for education, Epadhai began as a small initiative to provide free coding resources to students. Over the years, we have grown into a global learning platform offering courses, quizzes, and resources across various subjects. Our goal has always been to bridge the gap between learners and quality education by leveraging technology.

We believe education is the most powerful tool for change, and by making it accessible, we are shaping a better future for all.
